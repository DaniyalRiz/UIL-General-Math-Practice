Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

UIL Math Practice ("the site," "we," "us") is a free practice platform for UIL General Mathematics. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it's handled.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google to create and identify your account. We do not receive or store your Google password.
  • Practice activity. We store the questions you attempt, your answers, whether they were correct, and how long you took, so your progress and statistics can be saved across sessions.
  • Notes and community content. Any notes, community-submitted solutions, votes, question reports, or bug reports you choose to submit are stored under your account.

How we use this information

Your information is used solely to operate the site: authenticating your account, saving your practice history and statistics, displaying community-submitted content, and improving the question bank based on reports. We do not sell your information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Where data is stored

Account and activity data is stored with Supabase, our backend database and authentication provider. Supabase acts as our data processor and does not use your data for its own purposes. Worked solutions and explanations for some questions are generated using Anthropic's Claude API; that process does not involve your personal account information.

Your choices

You can request a copy of your data, or request that your account and all associated data (attempts, notes, reports, votes, and community solutions) be permanently deleted, by emailing us at the address below. We will act on deletion requests promptly.

Children's privacy

UIL Math Practice is intended for students preparing for UIL competitions, who may be minors. We only collect the information described above (Google account basics and practice activity) and do not knowingly collect any other personal information from anyone, including children.
